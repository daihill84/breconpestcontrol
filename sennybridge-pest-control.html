Sennybridge and the Brecon Beacons are home to a thriving Red Kite population. Because these birds are scavengers, they are at extreme risk of secondary poisoning from rodenticides.

To ensure success: All bird feeding must stop 24 hours before our arrival. Rats will ignore traps if bird seed is present. If feeders remain active on arrival, a call-out/survey fee will apply as treatment cannot safely proceed.
