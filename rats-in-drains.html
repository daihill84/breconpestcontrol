Why Choose Stainless Steel Rat Blockers?

Plastic "one-way" valves are easily chewed through by rats. We only install high-quality 316-grade stainless steel blockers. These allow waste to flow out but prevent rats from climbing up from the main sewer into your property's pipework.

  • Permanent, non-toxic solution.
  • No ongoing chemical costs.
  • Stops rats before they reach your cavity walls or attic.
  • Safe for all modern and heritage drainage systems.
