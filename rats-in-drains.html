Common Questions: Rats & Drainage

How do rats get from the drains into my house?

Rats are expert swimmers and can climb vertical pipes. They look for faults like cracked clay pipes, redundant junctions, or displaced joints to exit the sewer system and enter your wall cavities.

What is a CCTV drain survey for rats?

David uses a high-definition drainage camera specifically to look for 'rat runs' and structural defects that standard plumbers might miss.

Will a rat blocker stop rats permanently?

Yes. We install Grade 316 Stainless Steel Rat Blockers. These allow waste to flow out but prevent rats from swimming back up into your property.
