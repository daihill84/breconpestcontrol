UK Law: The Truth About Squirrel Relocation

In the UK, it is strictly illegal to relocate grey squirrels. Under the Wildlife and Countryside Act 1981, releasing a trapped grey squirrel back into the wild is a criminal offence. Relocation only moves the problem and causes immense stress to the animal.

We provide a 100% legal service. We catch grey squirrels using professional Fenn and spring traps housed in secure tunnels. Once caught, they are humanely dispatched on-site following strict welfare standards. We solve the problem where national firms often fail.
