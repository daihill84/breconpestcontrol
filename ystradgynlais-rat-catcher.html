Technical Rat Proofing & Drainage Solutions

Many rodent issues in Ystradgynlais, particularly around Gough Road and Wern-Plemys, are linked to the aging drainage infrastructure. Rats are highly skilled at navigating sewers and can enter wall cavities through small cracks or redundant junctions in the pipework. This is often why homeowners hear scratching in the attic or walls but find no entry points at ground level.

I specialize in Rat Drainage Surveys and the installation of professional-grade stainless steel rat valves (non-return valves). These devices allow waste to leave your property but prevent rats from entering. This is the only permanent way to stop "sewer rats" from infesting terraced and semi-detached homes in SA9.
