Our "Proofing First" Method

The most humane way to handle wildlife is to prevent entry in the first place. David provides comprehensive structural surveys to identify:

  • Gaps in rooflines used by squirrels and birds.
  • Low-level entry points for hedgehogs and rodents.
  • Defective drainage allowing rats to enter wall cavities.

By using heavy-duty steel mesh and permanent sealants, we create a physical barrier that makes lethal control unnecessary.
