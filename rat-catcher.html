Rat Control for Houses & Homes

We understand how distressing it is to find rats in your home. Scratching in the loft, droppings in the kitchen or gnawing noises at night – we deal with it every day.

We always start with non-toxic trapping, but if the infestation is severe we are fully licensed and certificated (RSPH/Lantra Level 2) to use professional rodenticides safely and legally in domestic properties.

Rat Control for Farms, Barns & Silage Stores

Sheep, beef and dairy farmers trust us weekly across Powys and Carmarthenshire. We prefer 100% traditional trapping (Fenn, T-Rex, snap traps) because it’s safe for livestock, dogs and silage.

However, when numbers are extremely high or rats are resistant, we are fully licensed and trained to deploy professional rodenticide bait stations in the correct, legal, locked boxes – zero risk to stock or dogs.

Fully Licensed & Safe Use of Rodenticides

We hold the RSPH / Lantra Level 2 Award in Pest Management – Safe Use of Rodenticides and follow the Campaign for Responsible Rodenticide Use (CRRU) code at all times.

This means when the toughest jobs require baiting, you are 100% legally and safely covered – something many smaller operators cannot offer.
