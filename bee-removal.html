Bee Control Services

At Welsh Town And Country Pest Services, we prioritize the protection of bees, recognizing their crucial role in our ecosystem. Our professional bee control services in Brecon and Port Talbot are designed to handle bee-related issues effectively while ensuring the safety of these vital pollinators.

Safe Relocation of Beehives

We understand that bees should only be killed under specific circumstances, such as when they pose an immediate threat to human safety. Our primary focus is to relocate beehives safely. Our expert team will assess the situation and provide solutions that allow us to move the hive without harming the bees.

  • Immediate Response: We are available 24/7 to address your bee-related concerns.
  • Comprehensive Services: From inspections to hive relocations, we cover all aspects of bee control.
