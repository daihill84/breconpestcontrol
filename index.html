Professional Rat Control & Drain Surveys in Brecon

Rodent infestations are more than just a nuisance; they are a significant health risk and can cause structural damage to your property. In Brecon and the surrounding LD3 areas, rats often find their way into homes through compromised drainage systems. As an Ex-Pestokil technician, I specialize in identifying the root cause of the problem rather than just scratching the surface.

I utilize advanced CCTV drain surveys to locate "blind junctions" or cracked pipes where rats exit the sewer system and enter your wall cavities or lofts. My approach is poison-free whenever possible, focusing on permanent proofing and non-toxic trapping. This ensures that your pets and local wildlife are kept safe while the infestation is dealt with effectively.

Traditional Mole Trapping Across South Wales

Moles can destroy a manicured lawn or a productive farm field in a matter of days. While many companies rely on gassing, which is often ineffective in the porous Welsh soil, I use traditional trapping methods. This is a skill honed over decades, ensuring a high catch rate and a quick resolution to your mole hill problem.

Whether you have a single mole in a garden in Crickhowell or a major infestation across acreage in the Brecon Beacons, I provide a "no catch, no fee" or fixed-price service depending on the scale. My agricultural background makes me the preferred choice for local farmers who need their land cleared of mounds that can damage machinery and contaminate silage.

Squirrel Control & Wasp Nest Removal

Grey squirrels are an invasive species that can cause thousands of pounds in damage by gnawing on electrical wiring and timber joists in lofts. I offer comprehensive squirrel proofing and removal services in Sennybridge, Talgarth, and Bwlch. Once the squirrels are removed, I ensure the entry points are sealed with professional-grade materials to prevent future re-entry.

During the summer months, wasp nests become a primary concern. I provide a rapid-response service for wasp nest removal. I use professional-grade insecticides that are not available to the public, ensuring the nest is neutralized in a single visit. If you have an emergency near a public area or a school, I prioritize these calls to ensure public safety.
