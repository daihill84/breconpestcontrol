The Extraction Process: Safety & Conservation

Honeybees (Apis mellifera) are essential to our ecosystem. When a swarm chooses a domestic or commercial building, it can cause structural concerns and safety risks. Our RSPH-certified team uses vacuum-assisted live capture and thermal imaging to locate the cluster without unnecessary damage to your property. By choosing ethical removal, you prevent the "feral colony" issues that occur when bees are killed but the honey and wax are left behind to rot or attract other pests.
